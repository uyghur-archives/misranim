Dear Sir/Madam,

Christmas and New Year Greetings from IIHMR!

As you may be aware, the Institute of Health Management Research (IIHMR) launched its Post Graduate Diploma in Rural Management (PGDRM) in 2010.  As the first batch of this programme prepares to graduate and embrace the challenges facing India’s volatile rural sector, it is with immense pleasure that the School of Rural Management, IIHMR would like to invite you to participate our national conference on the subject of “Livelihood Challenges in a Globalizing India” on January 16-17, 2012. This event, named “Goonj” is our attempt to bring together prominent voices engaged with policy, entrepreneurship, and research on issues related to livelihood generation in the country.



The IIHMR, India’s premier institute working on issues of health management and research has had a long and abiding commitment to an engagement with the problems associated with India’s evolving rural landscape.  For almost three decades, the institute has sought to resolve the dilemma between explicit value orientation towards the betterment of the poor and the value neutral optimization approach of conventional management education.  Towards this end, IIHMR started its programme in Rural Management with the intention of preparing professionals for a challenging career in managerial positions in the public and private sectors, as well as in NGOs, and national and multinational organizations.




        This conference mainly aims

·      To exchange, deliberate and discuss ideas to negotiate the challenges of providing livelihoods in a just, sustainable and equitable manner.  

·      To bring effective solutions for meeting the livelihood needs of India’s large and diverse population

















In this regard, a detailed brochure and registration form is enclosed herewith.We thereby extend a hearty invitation to you to be the part of our conference, and shall be highly obliged if you could accept our invitation.



I look forward to hearing from you at your earliest

Yours sincerely



Prof.Goutam Sadhu

Conference Organizing Secretary and

Associate Dean

School of Rural Management

IIHMR Jaipur
